A Wife, a Twin, & a Friend to All My Journey

I believe that developing and maintaining a growth mindset is key to catalyzing necessary changes in our own lives and in the world, and I believe that both formal and informal education are some of the most powerful tools we can use in our efforts to protect and enable both humankind and the environment.

Utilizing my skills of creativity, design, and communication, I hope to be an advocate for peace and charity towards all people; however, I am particularly passionate about helping individuals and businesses through teaching and promoting financial literacy.

From my involvement in multiple start-ups and non-profits I know the importance of prioritizing the creation of a viable business model and making smart investments with time and money.

I have also seen individuals and families ravaged by the lack of understanding or lack of ability to wisely accrue, save, use, and invest their money.

I hope to promote better preventative healthcare, stronger faith and spirituality, kindness, charity, and careful planning and decision-making by teaching and counseling individuals and businesses about their finances.

Rather than be controlled by money or our love of money, I hope we can improve the quality of our lives and the lives of others less fortunate than us by learning to control and use our money wisely.

To have the adequate knowledge and skillset to accomplish these goals, I have returned to school to finish my B.S. of Finance at BYU.
